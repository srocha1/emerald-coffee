Our Story

Emerald Coffee was born from a dream — a dream to bring the richness of Colombia's land and heritage into every cup of coffee.

I'm Sofia Rocha, a Colombian student and entrepreneur. I started Emerald Coffee with the support of my family in the Amazon region of Colombia. Our beans are more than just a product — they’re a reflection of who we are and where we come from.

Every bean is handpicked with love, slow-roasted with care, and packed to deliver a premium taste that honors our traditions.

Emerald Coffee isn't just about caffeine. It's about legacy, pride, and connection to nature — just like the emeralds of Colombia that inspired our name.
