Brewing Tips

Make the most of your Emerald Coffee with these expert tips:

  • Use filtered water: Coffee is 98% water — quality matters!
  • Grind just before brewing: For the freshest flavor, grind beans right before use.
  • Use the right ratio: Start with 2 tablespoons of ground coffee per 6 oz of water.
  • Espresso machine? Use fine grind, tamp evenly, and preheat your machine for best results.
  • Store beans properly: Keep them sealed, in a cool dark place — not in the fridge!
